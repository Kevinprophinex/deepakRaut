About

A dream inherited,
a practice built by hand.

Deepak Tukaram Raut has been practicing law in Mumbai since 2002. It began as someone else's dream — his father had always hoped that at least one of his children would become an advocate. Deepak took up that hope, and over time, it grew into something entirely his own: a genuine passion for the law, and for the work of standing beside people when they need it most.

What started as an inherited aspiration became more than two decades of independent practice across Mumbai and Thane — drafting, arguing, and appearing in matters ranging from the trial courts to the High Court of Judicature at Bombay.

"The law was his father's wish before it was his passion — today, it is simply who he is."
